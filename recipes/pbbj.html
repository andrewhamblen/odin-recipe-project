Andrew's Peanut Butter Banana and Jelly

Description

Andrew's Peanut Butter Banana and Jelly Sanwich was a classic for Andrew during his childhood. It was created out of necesity during those long summer days.

Andrew wanted to create a new sandwich based on an American classic; the PB&J. Thus, Andrew's Peanut Butter Banana and Jelly was born.

Ingredients

  • Oat Nut Whole Grain Sliced Bread
  • Skippy Super Chunk Peanut Butter
  • Organic Banana
  • Welch's Concord Grape Jelly
  • Any Sweet Organic Honey
    Steps
  1. Lather one slice of bread with skippy super chunk peanut butter
  2. Lather one slice of bread with welch's concord grape jelly
  3. Slice your organic banana into individual pieces and place over the peanut butter lathered bread
  4. Drizzle honey over the Jelly lathered slice of bread
  5. Bring the two slices of bread together
