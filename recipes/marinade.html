Andrew's Steak Marinade

Description

Andrew's Steak Marinade was developed after an encounter with a Washington State monk named Trevor.

It is made with a combination of herbs and spices with a base of soy sauce. Andrew's Steak Marinade is his own creation inspired by the one they call Trevor.

Ingredients

  • Plastic Bag
  • Soy Sauce
  • Mango Habanero Sauce
  • Siracha Sauce
  • Red Pepper Flakes
  • Freeze Dried Chives
  • Salt
  • Pepper
    Steps
  1. Add 1 dash of soy sauce into a plastic bag
  2. Add 2 squeezes of mango habanero
  3. Add 2 squirts of siracha sauce
  4. Add 1 shake of red pepper flakes
  5. Add 1 shake of freeze dried chives
  6. Add 3 shakes of salt and pepper both
  7. Add steak and let sit for 3-24 hours
